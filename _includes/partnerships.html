Our Partners

Greenlight is proud to work with the following organizations.

Curamericas has provided grants to schools throughout North Carolina to use Greenlight fo free. Additionally, Curamericas has provided community health workers who handle social service requests for Greenlight families ranging from food assistance to income relief.

Along with partner with Duke Health's top physicians for population health and infectious diseases, Greenlight has partnered with Duke to provide free testing to the Durham community. Duke is also processing COVID-19 relief payments for those who need to quarantine.

Through >Supply Hawk, Greenlight provides low cost access to personal protective equipment for both school staff and family.

Stop the Spread has brought together a community of executives, volunteers, donors, and subject matter experts to catalyze action and augment government in response to COVID-19.
