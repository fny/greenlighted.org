How does Greenlight work?

  • Parents Greenlight Students

    Every morning parents submit a quick survey with their children's symptoms.

  • Connect with Care

    We connect symptomatic students to testing and care services.

  • Recover

    Along with guiding the students recovery, we share our data with the local health department so they can improve their tracing efforts to keep everyone safe.

Protect What Matters Most

Greenlight offers clinical oversight for all schools. In select areas, Greenlight can directly connect families with clinical and social support.

  • Greenlight has partnered with North Carolina to provide eligible schools access to county and state health care workers.
  • Greenlight is working with other states to provide care services to schools as well.
  • Expert clinical oversight and educational guidance from our team for all schools.
